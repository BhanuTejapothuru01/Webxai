WHO WE ARE.

A digital engineering studio focused on AI, communication, identity, and full-stack product delivery.

We are a digital engineering studio obsessed with building products that feel inevitable — intelligent, fast, and unmistakably yours.

WEBXAI exists at the intersection of automation, communication, identity, and full-stack craft. We partner with founders and teams who refuse to ship mediocre work, and we treat every engagement like a long-term product — not a one-off deliverable.

From autonomous AI agents that eliminate repetitive ops, to WhatsApp flows that reach customers in seconds, to brand systems that hold up at scale — we engineer the full stack of what modern businesses actually need to grow.

What We Deliver

AI & Automation Custom LLM integrations, autonomous agents, RAG knowledge bases, and workflow automation. We connect AI to your product and internal tools so repetitive work disappears and your team focuses on high-value decisions.
WhatsApp API WhatsApp Business API setup, template messaging, conversational bots, OTP flows, and CRM integrations. Reach customers instantly on the world's most-used messaging platform — compliant, reliable, and built to convert.
Identity & Branding Logo design, Figma design systems, typography, color tokens, and brand guidelines. We craft identities that scale across websites, apps, marketing, and pitch materials without losing coherence.
Full Stack Applications React and Next.js frontends, Node.js APIs, PostgreSQL databases, authentication, and CI/CD deployment. From MVP to production systems handling real traffic — engineered with precision, not shortcuts.
